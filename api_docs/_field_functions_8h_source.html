25 /*
26  A note on design:
27 
28  FieldFunction methods can intersect the desired field (set of fields)
29  with the universal part as follows:
30 
31  const stk::mesh::Selector selector =
32  metaData.universal_part() &
33  stk::mesh::selectField(xField) & stk::mesh::selectField(yField);
34 
35  The above selector would include aura-entities
36 
37  An alternative choice would be to intersect the desired field )set of fields)
38  with the locally_owned or globally_shared part as follows:
39 
40  stk::mesh::Selector s_all_nodes
41  = (metaData.locally_owned_part() | metaData.globally_shared_part())
42  &stk::mesh::selectField(*myField);
43 
44  The above selector would exclude aura-entities
45 */
